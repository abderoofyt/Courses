Assalamu Alaikum 🌙

Welcome to Islamic Spice & Advice — where we mix joyful reminders with meaningful messages. Life can be spicy, but with a sprinkle of Islamic wisdom, every moment becomes a chance to grow closer to Allah ﷻ.

Whether you're sipping chai or stuck in traffic, there's always time for a good reminder!

Why I Love Sharing Islamic Advice

Giving naseehah (good advice) is a beautiful part of our deen. It helps build brotherhood and sisterhood, and keeps our hearts connected to Allah. Sharing it in a light and fun way makes it easier to remember and apply.

The Prophet ﷺ said: "Religion is sincere advice..." (Sahih Muslim)

Top Tips with a Dash of Deen

  • ✨ Begin everything with Bismillah – it adds barakah to even the smallest tasks!
  • 🌸 Say Alhamdulillah in both good and tough times – gratitude is powerful.
  • 🕊️ Smile – it’s a sunnah and sadaqah at the same time!
  • 📿 Make Dhikr while doing chores – laundry and tasbeeh go well together!
  • 📖 Read a small portion of Qur'an daily – even one ayah counts.

Bonus Wisdom 💡

"Verily, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest." – Qur'an (13:28)

"The best among you are those who learn the Qur'an and teach it." – Prophet Muhammad ﷺ

Never underestimate the power of a kind word or du'a. You never know whose heart it will heal.

May Allah guide us all and keep our hearts firm on the truth. 💖
